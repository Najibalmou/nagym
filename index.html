Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about our gym memberships, facilities, or classes? Here are some common questions and answers to help you get started at NA GYM.

1. What are the gym’s opening hours?

NA Gym is open from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM, Monday through Friday, and from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM on weekends.

2. Do I need to book classes in advance?

While many of our classes are drop-in, we recommend booking in advance for popular classes to ensure you get a spot. You can book classes through our website or app.

3. What should I bring for my first workout?

For your first workout, bring comfortable workout clothes, a water bottle, and a towel. If you have any specific fitness goals or needs, feel free to ask our staff for advice!

4. Are personal training sessions available?

Yes, we offer personal training sessions with certified trainers. You can schedule a session with a trainer at the front desk or through our website.

5. What is the cancellation policy for memberships?

Our membership cancellation policy varies depending on the plan. Please review the terms and conditions associated with your membership plan or contact our support team for more details.
